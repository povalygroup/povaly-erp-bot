📋 BOARDROOM GUIDE — MEETING NOTES & DECISIONS

🎯 PURPOSE: Record meeting notes, decisions, and action items.

📝 MEETING NOTES FORMAT

[MEETING] Meeting title
[DATE] YYYY-MM-DD
[TIME] HH:MM - HH:MM
[ATTENDEES] @user1, @user2, @user3
[AGENDA] What was discussed
[DECISIONS] What was decided
[ACTION_ITEMS] Who does what by when
[NEXT_MEETING] YYYY-MM-DD (if scheduled)

Example:
[MEETING] Q2 Planning
[DATE] 2026-04-28
[TIME] 10:00 - 11:30
[ATTENDEES] @ceo, @manager1, @lead.dev
[AGENDA] Q2 goals, resources, timeline
[DECISIONS] Focus on mobile, hire 2 devs, launch by June 30
[ACTION_ITEMS]
• @manager1: Hiring plan by May 5
• @lead.dev: Tech roadmap by May 3
• @ceo: Budget approval by May 1
[NEXT_MEETING] 2026-05-12

🔄 ACTION ITEM TRACKING

Reactions track status:
👍 = ACKNOWLEDGED (I'll do this)
🔄 = IN_PROGRESS (Working on it)
❤️ = COMPLETED (Finished)
👎 = BLOCKED (Need help)

⚡ COMMANDS

/meetings - View all notes
/meeting TITLE - View full notes
/myactions - View your action items
/actionitems - View all items (managers)
/actionstatus - View status
/postmeeting - Post new notes

👑 FOR ORGANIZERS

WORKFLOW:
1. Schedule meeting
2. Take notes
3. Record decisions
4. Assign action items
5. Post notes within 24 hours
6. Track progress
7. Follow up

⚠️ Important:
• Post notes quickly
• Be specific about items
• Include clear deadlines
• Assign to specific people
• Follow up on progress

👷 FOR EMPLOYEES

WORKFLOW:
1. Attend meeting
2. Participate actively
3. Review notes after
4. Acknowledge action items (👍)
5. Update status as you progress (🔄)
6. Mark complete (❤️)
7. Report results

⚠️ Important:
• Attend on time
• Participate actively
• Review notes
• Acknowledge items
• Complete by deadline

📊 DECISION TRACKING

Decisions tracked for accountability:
• Decision text
• Made by
• Date
• Status (ACTIVE, COMPLETED, CANCELLED, REVISED)

⚠️ TOPIC RULES

✓ DO: Post meeting notes, use format, include details, assign items
✗ DON'T: Post non-meeting messages, skip items, modify format
