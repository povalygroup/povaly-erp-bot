📋 BOARDROOM GUIDE — MEETING INVITATIONS & NOTES

🎯 PURPOSE: Schedule meetings and record meeting notes, decisions, and action items.

📅 MEETING INVITATION FORMAT

[MEETING_ID] MTG260501
[MEETING_INVITE] Meeting title
[DATE] YYYY-MM-DD
[TIME] HH:MM - HH:MM GMT+6
[DURATION] X hours / X minutes
[LOCATION] Zoom link / Physical location / Telegram call
[ORGANIZER] @username
[ATTENDEES] @user1, @user2, @user3
[AGENDA]
• Topic 1
• Topic 2
• Topic 3
[PREPARATION] What to prepare (optional)
[PRIORITY] LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH / URGENT

MEETING ID:
• Format: MTGYYMMXX (e.g., MTG260501)
• Leave blank for auto-generation
• System auto-corrects if duplicate/invalid
• Use /newmeeting for pre-filled template

SPECIAL ATTENDEE GROUPS:
• @all - Everyone (including on leave)
• @allactives - Active users only
• @employees - All employees
• @activeemployees - Active employees only
• @admins, @managers, @owners - Role-based

AUTOMATED REMINDERS:
System sends reminders at 24h, 1h, and 15m before meeting

RSVP (Attendance Confirmation):
React to meeting invitation to confirm your attendance

RSVP REACTIONS:
👍 = I will attend
❤️ = I will attend and I'm prepared
👎 = I cannot attend
🔥 = I have an urgent conflict

📝 MEETING NOTES FORMAT

[MEETING] Meeting title
[DATE] YYYY-MM-DD
[TIME] HH:MM - HH:MM
[ATTENDEES] @user1, @user2, @user3
[AGENDA] What was discussed
[DECISIONS] What was decided
[ACTION_ITEMS] Who does what by when
[NEXT_MEETING] YYYY-MM-DD (if scheduled)

Example:
[MEETING] Q2 Planning
[DATE] 2026-04-28
[TIME] 10:00 - 11:30
[ATTENDEES] @ceo, @manager1, @lead.dev
[AGENDA] Q2 goals, resources, timeline
[DECISIONS] Focus on mobile, hire 2 devs, launch by June 30
[ACTION_ITEMS]
• @manager1: Hiring plan by May 5
• @lead.dev: Tech roadmap by May 3
• @ceo: Budget approval by May 1
[NEXT_MEETING] 2026-05-12

🔄 ACTION ITEM TRACKING

Reactions track status:
👍 = ACKNOWLEDGED (I'll do this)
🔄 = IN_PROGRESS (Working on it)
❤️ = COMPLETED (Finished)
👎 = BLOCKED (Need help)

⚡ COMMANDS

/newmeeting - Get meeting invitation template
/meetings - View all meetings/notes
/mymeetings - View meetings you're invited to
/meeting <ID or title> - View meeting details
/myactions - Your action items from meetings
/actionitems - All action items (managers)
/actionstatus - View action item status
/postmeeting - Get meeting notes template
/cancelmeeting MTG-XXXX-XX - Cancel a meeting

👑 FOR ORGANIZERS

WORKFLOW:
1. Schedule meeting
2. Take notes
3. Record decisions
4. Assign action items
5. Post notes within 24 hours
6. Track progress
7. Follow up

⚠️ Important:
• Post notes quickly
• Be specific about items
• Include clear deadlines
• Assign to specific people
• Follow up on progress

👷 FOR EMPLOYEES

WORKFLOW:
1. Attend meeting
2. Participate actively
3. Review notes after
4. Acknowledge action items (👍)
5. Update status as you progress (🔄)
6. Mark complete (❤️)
7. Report results

⚠️ Important:
• Attend on time
• Participate actively
• Review notes
• Acknowledge items
• Complete by deadline

📊 DECISION TRACKING

Decisions tracked for accountability:
• Decision text
• Made by
• Date
• Status (ACTIVE, COMPLETED, CANCELLED, REVISED)

⚠️ TOPIC RULES

✓ DO: Post meeting notes, use format, include details, assign items
✗ DON'T: Post non-meeting messages, skip items, modify format
